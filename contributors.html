How to Contribute

Share your notes, sheets, previous questions & class materials. It takes just 3 minutes!

1

Create GitHub Account

Go to github.com/signup and create a free account.

2

Fork the Repository

Click Fork on the QSIS-ARMS repo.

3

Upload Your Academic Files

In your fork, go to upload_academic_files/{semester}/ and add your files.

4

Submit Pull Request

Open a Pull Request and fill in your Full Name, QSIS ID, and WhatsApp.

View Existing PRs
5

Get Credit!

Once merged, your name and avatar appear in this Contributors list automatically!
