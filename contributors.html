How to Contribute

Share your notes, sheets, previous questions & class materials with fellow students. It takes just 3 minutes!

1

Create GitHub Account

Go to github.com/signup and create a free account.

2

Fork the Repository

Click Fork on the QSIS-ARMS repo to copy it to your account.

3

Upload Your Academic Files

In your fork, go to upload_academic_files/{semester}/ and add your files. Place them in the correct category:

upload_academic_files/
  {semester}/ (e.g. 6th-semister)
    sheet/{course-code}/ — Study sheets
    Previous Question ({semester})/2026/Mid/
    NOTES/ — Lecture notes
    Syllabus/ — Course syllabi
4

Submit Pull Request

Open a Pull Request from your fork. Fill in your Full Name, QSIS ID, and WhatsApp. The bot will verify and auto-merge!

View Existing PRs
5

Get Credit!

Once merged, your name and avatar appear in this Contributors list automatically. You also help hundreds of QSIS students!
