Contribute Files

1

Login with GitHub

Click the Login button and connect your GitHub account. Verify with your IIUC email (q{number}@ugrad.iiuc.ac.bd).

2

Select Files

Choose your academic files (PDF, DOC, XLS, PPT, images). Max 10 files, 50MB total.

3

Fill in Details

Select semester & category, enter your Name, QSIS ID, and WhatsApp number.

4

Submit!

We automatically fork the repo, commit your files, and create a Pull Request. You'll see the PR link when done.
