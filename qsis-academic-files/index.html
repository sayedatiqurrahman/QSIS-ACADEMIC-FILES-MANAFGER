How to Upload Files

1

Click "Upload File"

Click the upload button in the top-right corner of the page.

2

Select Your File(s)

Choose PDF, DOC, DOCX, or image files from your computer.

3

Fill in Details

Select semester, category (sheet/question/note), year, and optional course code.

4

Submit for Review

Our team reviews and approves files. Approved files appear in the browser.
